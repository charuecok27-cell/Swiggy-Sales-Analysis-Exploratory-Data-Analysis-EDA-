# Swiggy-Sales-Analysis-Exploratory-Data-Analysis-EDA-
This project presents an exploratory data analysis (EDA) of sales data from Swiggy, aimed at uncovering key business insights through well-defined KPIs and visual analytics. The analysis focuses on understanding sales performance, customer behavior, and geographic trends to support data-driven decision-making.

# Key KPIs Analyzed:
Total Sales – Overall revenue performance
Total Orders – Platform demand and transaction volume
Average Order Value (AOV) – Customer spending behavior
Average Rating & Rating Count – Customer satisfaction and engagement

# Visual Insights:
Monthly and daily sales trends
Sales distribution by food type (Veg / Non-Veg)
State-wise and city-wise sales performance
Quarterly sales summary
Top 5 cities contributing to total revenue

# Key Takeaways
The analysis highlights Swiggy as a high-volume and operationally stable platform, with strong demand concentration in major urban regions and consistent ordering patterns over time. Variations across food categories and locations provide actionable insights for marketing, logistics, and expansion strategies.
